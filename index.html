The Balance of Life

Activity 1/10
⚖️

For people with Parkinson’s disease, staying active is more than just a physical challenge. It is a constant negotiation with social and emotional barriers. In this game, you step into that unseen struggle.

Every choice you make is a trade-off between your physical strength, your emotional well-being, and your social life. Your goal is to navigate 10 daily activities by swiping through choices. But beware: if your physical, social, or emotional bars hit 0%, you’ve reached your limit. Can you find the rhythm of a balanced life?
